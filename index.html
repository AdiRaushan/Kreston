FAQs

Kreston Menon strictly conducts audits in accordance with International Standards on Auditing (ISA) and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS), ensuring compliance with the UAE Commercial Companies Law.
UAE free zone company formation involves choosing a jurisdiction (e.g., DMCC, JAFZA, Hamriyah), registering the trade name, applying for the corporate activity license, and completing immigration and corporate bank account arrangements. Kreston Menon manages this process end-to-end.
We provide full support including initial registration, assessing corporate structures, determining taxable income thresholds, implementing transfer pricing master files, and preparing and submitting annual tax returns to the FTA.

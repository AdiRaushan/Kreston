About Kreston SVP

At Kreston SVP, we believe that trust is the foundation of every successful business. As one of the leading audit firms in Qatar, we provide expert financial and consulting solutions tailored for startups, SMEs, and large corporations.

We are proud to be part of Kreston Global, a worldwide network of independent accounting firms operating in over 120 countries. This global reach, combined with our deep local knowledge of Qatar's regulatory environment, ensures services that meet international standards while aligning with Qatar's compliance framework.

Whether you need financial audits, tax advisory services, business restructuring, or company formation in Qatar, Kreston SVP is your trusted partner.

FAQs

Kreston SVP strictly conducts audits in accordance with International Standards on Auditing (ISA) and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS), ensuring compliance with Qatar Commercial Companies Law.
Qatar company formation involves registering with the Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MOCI), obtaining the Commercial Registration (CR) registry, securing corporate licenses, and completing tax registry registration with the GTA. Kreston SVP manages this process end-to-end.
We provide full support including initial registration on the Dhareeba portal, assessing corporate structures, determining taxable income exemptions, implementing transfer pricing compliance, and preparing and submitting annual tax returns to the General Tax Authority (GTA).
